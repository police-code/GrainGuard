Post-Harvest Handling Tips

  • **Dry grains thoroughly** to reduce moisture levels and prevent mold growth.
  • **Clean storage facilities** to remove leftover residues that can harbor pests.
  • **Inspect grains regularly** for signs of infestation or spoilage.
  • **Use airtight containers** or sacks to reduce exposure to pests and humidity.
  • **Maintain proper ventilation** to prevent heat buildup and ensure air circulation.
  • **Store grains off the ground** to avoid moisture absorption from the floor.
  • **Apply appropriate pest control measures** before and during storage.
  • **Monitor temperature and humidity levels** to maintain optimal storage conditions.
  • **Avoid mixing new harvests with old grains** to reduce contamination risk.
  • **Label and track storage dates** for effective inventory management.
